Critical Flood Safety Precautions & Care

Follow these mandatory guidelines issued by disaster response units during emergency flooding situations to minimize waterborne dangers and structural injuries.

✔️ Do's (Essential Care)

  • Boil Drinking Water: Always boil water or mix in chlorine tablets before use to destroy harmful waterborne pathogens.
  • Check Higher Ground: Evacuate to concrete upper floors or predefined community shelters immediately if water approaches.
  • Pack Emergency Bags: Keep cash, medications, identity cards, dry rations, and fresh batteries in a waterproof bag.
  • Disconnect Power Lines: Switch off the main household electricity board circuit to prevent accidental shock hazards.

❌ Don'ts (Strict Warnings)

  • Avoid Standing Water: Do not walk or swim through flooded channels; structural undercurrents can sweep away adults easily.
  • Do Not Drive: Avoid crossing submerged roads. Vehicles can lose traction and slide into deeper ditches.
  • Do Not Touch Electronics: Stay completely away from water-damaged appliances, power poles, and fallen wiring systems.
  • Ignore Rumors: Do not spread unverified panic. Follow updates solely via government radios or official control panels.
