Immediate Survival & Medical Care Guide

If rescue teams are delayed due to bad weather conditions, implement these self-preservation protocols inside your isolated structure:

🩹 Waterborne Health & Injury Care

  • Treating Open Wounds: Clean any floodwater-exposed cuts instantly with clean bottled water and soap. Apply antiseptic to prevent severe Leptospirosis infection.
  • Snake or Insect Bites: Keep the bitten limb completely still below heart level. Do not cut the wound. Tie a firm cloth bandage 2 inches above the bite and wait for medical evacuation.
  • Preventing Dehydration: Never drink unboiled tap water or rainwater collected near chemical plants. Use ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts) packets or mix 6 teaspoons of sugar and 0.5 teaspoon of salt in clean water.
  • Mental Calming Strategy: Keep stranded children engaged. Reassure them that emergency responders are processing requests systematically. Take slow, deep breaths to avoid panic-induced oxygen depletion.
